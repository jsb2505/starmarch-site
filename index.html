✦ StarMarch

Conquer the galaxy. One device.

StarMarch is a turn-based territory strategy game set across the stars — claim star systems, hold constellations for reinforcements, and outmanoeuvre your rivals. Play hotseat with friends on one device or against the built-in AI. Fully offline: no account, no internet needed to play.

Support

Having a problem, found a bug, or have a suggestion? Please open an issue — include your device model and OS version for bug reports.

Common questions

  • How do I restore my Premium purchase? Menu → Go Premium → Restore purchases (sign into the same Apple/Google account you bought with).
  • Are my saved games backed up? Saves live on your device only. Deleting the app deletes them.
  • How do I change my ad/consent choices? See the options in the privacy policy.
