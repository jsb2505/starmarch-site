# StarMarch

**Conquer the stars.** StarMarch is a Risk-style territory strategy game set across the
galaxy — claim star systems, hold constellations for reinforcements, and outmanoeuvre
your rivals. Play hotseat with friends on one device or against the built-in AI.
Fully offline: no account, no internet needed to play.

## Support

Having a problem, found a bug, or have a suggestion? Please
[open an issue](https://github.com/jsb2505/starmarch-site/issues) — include your device
model and OS version if it's a bug report.

**Common questions**

- **How do I restore my Premium purchase?** Menu → Go Premium → *Restore purchases*
  (make sure you're signed into the same Apple / Google account you bought with).
- **Are my saved games backed up?** Saves live on your device only. Deleting the app
  deletes them.
- **How do I change my ad/consent choices?** See the options in our
  [privacy policy](./privacy).
