StarMarch — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 July 2026

StarMarch is a strategy board game for iOS and Android, developed and published by an independent developer (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what data is processed when you play StarMarch.

The short version

  • StarMarch is an offline game. It has no accounts, no sign-in, and no backend of ours — your games, saves, and settings stay on your device.
  • We collect no personal data ourselves. The only data processing comes from two industry-standard SDKs: Google AdMob (advertising, free tier only) and RevenueCat (purchase handling).
  • Buying Premium removes all advertising — and with it, all advertising-related data processing.

Data processed by third-party SDKs

Google AdMob (advertising — free tier only)

When you play the free tier, ads are served by Google AdMob, which may process:

  • Advertising identifiers (Apple IDFA / Google Advertising ID) — only with your consent (see “Your choices”); otherwise ads are served non-personalised;
  • Coarse location derived from your IP address;
  • Ad interaction diagnostics (impressions, clicks, performance).

See Google’s Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from partner apps.

RevenueCat (purchases)

If you buy Premium (or restore a purchase), RevenueCat processes your store purchase receipt and a random, anonymous app-user identifier to validate the purchase and maintain your entitlement. No name, email, or payment details reach RevenueCat or us — payment is handled entirely by Apple / Google. See RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy.

Your choices

  • Consent (GDPR / UK GDPR): in the EEA and UK you are shown a consent form before any ads load. If you decline, you still get the full game with non-personalised ads. You can revisit your choice from the ad’s “Ad choices” / privacy options.
  • iOS App Tracking Transparency: iOS asks separately whether StarMarch may track. If you decline, ads remain non-personalised. Change this in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Android: reset or delete your Advertising ID in Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Remove ads entirely: the one-time Premium purchase disables all advertising and all advertising-related processing.

Children

StarMarch is not directed at children under 13 and is age-rated accordingly (12+ / Teen).

Data retention & security

We store nothing about you on any server of ours. On-device data (saved games, settings) can be removed at any time by deleting the app. Data processed by Google and RevenueCat is retained under their policies linked above.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app or its SDKs change. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions or requests: please open an issue or use the support contact shown on the app’s store listing.
